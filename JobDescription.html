TITLE: WEB DEVELOPER

Salary: £57 723
Hours: 40 hours a week

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILTIES

DUTIES

- Creating and maintaining databases to store website content and user data

- Optimising websites and web applications for performance and SEO.

- Testing and debugging websites and application to ensure they are working correctly.

RESPONSIBILITIES

- writing clean, efficient, and well documented code

- Implement and maintain the websites.

- trouble shooting and resolving technical issues.

SKILLS

- strong problem solving and analytical skills.

- Ability to work independently and manage multiple project simultaneously..

- Proficiency in programming languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCES

- QUALIFICATION

- Bachelor's degree in computer science

- EXPERIENCE

- Building websites and web application using programming languages and frameworks.

- Strong understanding of web development principle as well as experience with control system web development tools and database management.

TITLE: PROJECT MANAGER

Salary: £58 723
Hours: 40 hours a week

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILTIES

DUTIES
- Monitoring the progress of a project, tracking milestones, and identifying and addressing any issues or risk that arises.

- Budget and tracking expenses

- Quality control: ensure that projects meet quality standards, and that deliverable are completed to the satisfaction of stakeholders.

RESPONSIBILITIES

- Developing detailed project plan, including timeline, budgets, and resume requirements

- Managing project resources

- Coordinating project activities and ensuring that all team members are working together effectively.

- Communicating with stakeholder, including clients, team member, and executives, to keep them informed of project progress and to address any concerns or question.

Skills

- Risk management- must be able to identify and manage risks associated with the projects, including potential delays, cost, overruns, and other issues that could impact the project’s success.

- Organisation- highly organised and able to take responsibilities simultaneous.

- Leadership- able to inspire and motivate employees.

- Negotiation- must be able to negotiate with stakeholder to resolve conflicts.

- adaptability

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCES

QUALIFICATIONS

- Bachelor's degree in business, engineering, or computer science

- OR Project management professional (PMP) certification

EXPERIENCE

- 2 or 3 years of working in the industry.

- Strong understanding of technical aspects of the projects

- Leadership experiences

TITLE: DESIGNER

Salary: £49 723
Hours: 30 hours a week

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILTIES

DUTIES
- Creating visual designs that are aesthetically pleasing, user-friendly, and consistent with brand guidance.

- Communicate design concept and decision to client and stakeholder.

- Staying up to date with the latest design trends, tool and technologies.

- Ensure that designs are accessible to users with disabilities.

RESPONSIBILITIES

- Ensuring that designs are optimised for search engine visibility and performance.

- Managing multiple projects and deadline simultaneously.

- Collaborating with developers to ensure that designs are implemented correctly and function as intended.

Skills

- User experience design

- Collaboration

- Time management

- Communication

- Search engine optimisation- designers should have basic understanding of SEO principle to ensure that designs are optimised for search engine visibility.

QUALIFICATIONS AND EXPERIENCES

- QUALIFICATION
- Degree or diploma in related field such as graphics designs, web designs or computer science

- EXPERIENCE

- Working in a professional setting, freelancer, or part of a design team.

- Industry knowledge- good understanding of the latest designs trends and technologies.
